Basketball: A Global Sport

Basketball is one of the most popular and dynamic sports in the world, known for its fast-paced action, teamwork, and athleticism. The game is played between two teams of five players each, aiming to score points by shooting a ball through the opponent's hoop. Invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith, basketball has grown into a global phenomenon, played and watched by millions.

Key Features of Basketball:

  • Objective: Score points by getting the ball through the opposing team's basket.
  • Court: A rectangular court with a hoop at each end, typically indoors.
  • Game Duration: Professional games consist of four 12-minute quarters.
  • Players: Each team has 5 players on the court: two guards, two forwards, and one center.
  • Scoring: A shot made from outside the three-point line is worth 3 points, within the line is 2 points, and free throws are worth 1 point.
