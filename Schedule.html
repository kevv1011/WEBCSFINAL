MAKING A SCHEDULE?

Organize Your Basketball Season: Easy Steps
to Create and Manage Your Game Schedule

1. Define your Coaching Goals:

Establish clear objectives for each practice and meeting. Determine what skills, strategies, or team-building activities you want to focus on.

2. Set Practice Dates and Times:

Choose regular practice times that fit with players' availability and venue access. For example:

  • Practice Session 1: Mondays at 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM
  • Practice Session 2: Thursdays at 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM

3. Schedule Team Meetings:

Plan Meetings to discuss game strategies, player, progress, or team issues. Schedule these meetings before or after practice or on separate days.

  • Stategy Meeting: Wednesdays at 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM
  • Team Review MeetingL Fridays at 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM

4. Plan for Individual Player Sessions:

Include time slots for one-on-one sessions with palyers who need extra help or specific coaching.

  • Individual Training: Available by appointment on Tuesdays and Saturdays.
