BASKETBALL COACHING

Your Ultimate Destination for All Things Basketball — From Game Rules to Player Profiles, We've Got You Covered!

Where to Find Us? ⚲

We are located in the heart of Angeles City, Pampanga, offering
easy access to our basketball courts, training facilities, and coaching
programs. Whether you're joining us for games, practice, or events,
our location is convenient and well-equipped for all your basketball needs.

Key Features:

  • Indoor Basketball Court: Regulation-size court with seating for
    spectators.
  • Outdoor Courts: Available for open play and team practices.
  • Training Center: Fully equipped with weights, conditioning
    tools, and a recovery area.
  • Parking: Ample parking space available for players and visitors.
  • Accessibility: Our facility is wheelchair accessible and designed to
    accommodate all players and fans.

Opening Hours

  • Weekdays: 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • Weekends: 10: AM - 6:00 PM

Welcome to Hoop Haven

We are passionate about basketball and dedicated to bringing you the latest news, insights, and resources about the game we love.

Our Mission

At Hoops Haven, our mission is to connect basketball enthusiasts, players, coaches, and fans by providing comprehensive and engaging content about the sport. Whether you’re looking for game schedules, coaching tips, player profiles, or the latest basketball news, we strive to be your go-to source for everything basketball.
